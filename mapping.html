Mapping

Purpose:
Understand how Twitch fits into the history of livestreaming and 'social gaming' in order to generate new concepts for Twitch's platform.

Process:
Map Twitch into timelines of livestreaming and social gaming, create a comprehensive cause and effect map of the livestreaming and social gaming landscapes.

Payoff:
Deep insight into the circumstances that led to Twitch and concepts about where it could go next.

I am very interested in the history of communications technology and I believe that Twitch has a large role to play in the future of the industry. With this project I sought to map out the signals that led to Twitch in order to contextualize Twitch as a behaviour platform. This allowed me to generate new concepts for Twitch features inspired by the nature of the platform.

Purpose:
Understand the history of data and data collection in order to responsibly design with data as material going forward.

Process:
Map out cause and effect signals in the history of data, map out today's behaviour platform economy, predict the trends that will be made manifest in the Opt-In future.

Payoff:
Deep insight into the history of data as material and a strong prediction of what's to come next.

The above maps are on Data as Material, "A Culture of Moments" (Alexander Manu, Transforming Organizations for the Subscription Economy: Starting from Scratch Routledge, 2017) and my concept, The Opt-In Future.

Data as Material is the idea of data creation and collection serving as the basis of design and business activity today. Users produce and consume data which leads to the production and consumption of more data. This has reshaped business model design and creates new opprtunities for networks, services and experience optimization.

Data as Material produces A Culture of Moments. DaM reinvented conventional value propositions by putting the value delivery at the center of the experience. These new forms of value delivery are usually seamless and quick and they create ecosystems like the sharing and streaming economies and XaaS.

DaM and A Culture of Moments in tandem with the automation and augmentation of tasks is shaping the Opt-In Future, a future wherein humanity is freed from mundane tasks and able to focus on human pursuits. Automation and augmentation in the home and workplace already save us time and the Opt-In Future is all about who we will become during the time saved.
