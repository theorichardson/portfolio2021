Signals

Signals 2021 is a constantly evolving web page that I update with signals in technology and human behaviour that I observe or read about in my daily life.

Purpose:
Keep on top of signals at the intersection of technology and human behaviour.

Process:
Continually update Signals 2021 with signals I observe. I will organize the signals into innovation areas once the list is long enough.

Payoff:
Deep insight into where technology and our relationship with it is going. This site is a constant source of inspiration for concept generation.
